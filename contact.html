Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I purchase an account?

+
Simply browse our catalog, select the account you want, add it to your cart, and proceed through our secure checkout process. You'll receive account credentials via email within minutes of purchase.

Are the accounts verified and safe?

+
Yes! Every account on our platform goes through a thorough verification process. We check authenticity, engagement rates, and ensure all accounts meet our quality standards before listing.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

Is there a refund policy?

+
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your account, contact our support team and we'll process your refund.

How long does account transfer take?

+
Most account transfers are completed within 5-30 minutes after payment confirmation. You'll receive all necessary credentials and instructions via email immediately.
